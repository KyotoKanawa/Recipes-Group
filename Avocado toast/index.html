Avocado Toast

Recipe provided by Healthy Eats, found on https://www.healthyeats.com/

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, radishes, microgreens

Equipment

  • Toaster
  • Fork
  • Bowl

Directions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth. Add olive oil, salt, and pepper, and mix well.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread slices.
  5. Add optional toppings if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
